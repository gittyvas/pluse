Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

Pluse CRM allows you to fully delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: From Your Dashboard

1. Log into your Pluse CRM account.
2. Go to the Settings page.
3. Click Delete Account.
4. Confirm the deletion.

This will immediately delete your notes, reminders, and revoke access to your Google account data.

Option 2: By Request

If you cannot log in, email us at privacy@pluse.name.ng with the subject Account Deletion Request.

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm once your data is permanently removed.

Google Data Access Revocation

When you delete your account, Pluse CRM automatically revokes OAuth tokens from Google’s secure API. You may also manually revoke access from your Google account at any time:
https://myaccount.google.com/connections?filters=2
